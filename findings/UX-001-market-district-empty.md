# Market District is consistently empty of NPCs

## Observed
At turn 29, Market District has no NPCs present. It is described as the main commercial
hub but has no persistent inhabitants.

## Context
NPCs passing through (Mira, Finn) may stop there briefly during patrol, but none have
it as a frequent waypoint. No NPC has Market District as their primary location.

## Impact
Players traveling to the market find no one to interact with in a location that should
be the most populated place in town.

## Fix
Seed one additional NPC (e.g. a merchant or Guild informant) whose patrol prioritizes
Market District. Or give an existing NPC a routine that spends more time there.
